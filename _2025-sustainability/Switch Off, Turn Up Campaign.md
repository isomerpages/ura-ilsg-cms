<p><strong>Switch Off, Turn Up: Advancing sustainability at i Light Singapore</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>A cornerstone of the festivalâ€™s sustainability efforts since 2010, the <em>Switch Off, Turn Up&nbsp;</em> campaign
promotes energy conservation by encouraging building owners, corporations
and businesses to switch off non-essential lighting and set air conditioning
to more optimal temperatures throughout the festival duration.</p>
<p>Participating organisations are also encouraged to continue with these
energy-saving practices beyond the festival duration.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
