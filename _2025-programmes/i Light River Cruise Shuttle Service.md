<p><strong>i Light River Cruise Shuttle Service: Sail through the lights</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Experience i Light Singapore 2025 on the waters! Our river cruise shuttle
service connects visitors between Bayfront South Jetty, Clifford Pier Jetty
and Raffles Place Jetty seamlessly.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Marvel at illuminated artworks from unique angles as you glide across
Marina Bay and Singapore River on our eco-friendly vessels, capturing perfect
photos of the light installations set against Singapore's dazzling skyline.&nbsp;</p>
<p>With regular departures throughout the evening, this service is the most
hassle-free link between key festival locations, helping you make the most
of your festival experience.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Operated jointly by Singapore River Cruise and WaterB, this scenic shuttle
service adds another touch of magic to your journey as you witness this
captivating celebration of art, innovation and sustainability from the
heart of the bay!&nbsp;</p>
