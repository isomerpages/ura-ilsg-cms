<p><strong>Sunset Silent Flow: Flow and glow with stillness at sundown</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<p>In celebration of World Yoga Day, get ready to immerse yourself in a truly
unforgettable experience: a 90-minute <em>Sunset Silent Flow</em>, brought
to you by Silent Disco Asia and Yoga Movement, in collaboration with i
Light Singapore 2025.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Set against the iconic skyline at The Promontory, this exclusive session
invites you to flow, move, and breathe together as the sun dips below the
horizon - painting the sky in warm hues of gold and rose.&nbsp;</p>
<p>With Silent Disco Asia’s wireless headsets, your practice becomes even
more personal and immersive. Every cue. Every beat. Every breath - felt
more deeply, as you move through your flow amidst the tranquil buzz of
the city winding down.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This is more than just yoga. It’s an ambient fusion of light, motion,
and mindfulness, set in one of Singapore’s most stunning open-air locations.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>One Night. One Flow. One Unforgettable Sunset.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Spaces are limited - secure your spot now. You won’t want to miss this.&nbsp;</p>
