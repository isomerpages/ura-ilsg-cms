<p><strong>Silent Disco Parade: Groove in your own world together</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>As night falls over the glittering cityscape, experience a one-of-a-kind,
open-air dance procession through the heart of Marina Bay. With wireless
headphones playing feel-good music, you’ll become part of a glowing, kinetic
celebration weaving through the Festival’s stunning light art installations.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Visitors can look forward to the following:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>An immersive route: Journey through i Light Singapore’s curated light
art, pausing to dance and marvel at each breathtaking piece.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Skyline magic: From Marina Bay Sands, ArtScience Museum to The Promontory,
your backdrop is one of Asia’s most iconic urban nightscapes.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Crystal-clear audio: Dance to your own beat, all in perfect harmony with
your fellow parade-goers.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>With a limited number of places, this is a rare and intimate way to experience
this celebration of light and music.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This isn’t just a party – it’s a moving celebration of creativity, community
and the vibrant spirit of i Light Singapore.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tickets are limited – don’t miss your chance to dance in Marina Bay like
never before.&nbsp;</p>
